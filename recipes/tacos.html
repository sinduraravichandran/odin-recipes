Beef Tacos

Description

This is a healthy, high protein, high fiber beef taco recipe

Ingredients

  • Soft corn tacos
  • Olive oil
  • Ground beef 90%
  • Beef siete seasoning
  • Mexican shredded cheese
  • Avocado
  • Pickled onions
  • Black beans

Steps

  1. Heat 0.5 tsp of olive oil in a pan
  2. Add 5oz ground beef to the pan and mix with 2 tsp Siete seasoning
  3. After beef is cooked, add to tacos
  4. Top with Mexican shredded cheese, avocado, black beans, and pickled onions
