Burger

Description

This is a turkey burger recipe

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey patty
  • Olive oil
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Brioche bun
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat 0.5 tsp of olive oil in a pan
  2. Cook 5oz turkey patty in the pan with olive oil
  3. Toast bun and add turkey patty to bun
  4. Top with onion and cheese
