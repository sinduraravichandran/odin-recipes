Chicken

Description

This is a healthy, high protein, chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Olive oil
  • Sweet potato
  • Green beans
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat 0.5 tsp of olive oil in a pan
  2. Cook 5oz chicken in the pan with olive oil
  3. Bake 100g sweet potato in oven on 400 degrees
  4. Coat green beans in 0.5 tsp olive oil and air fry
